A2-GO!

Explore the famous town of trees, learn about important landmarks, and make the forest greener!

Immerse yourself in a digital world of money-making trees and mischievous squirrels! In this location-based game, you can plant a variety of tree species, protect your plants from squirrels, and discover important landmarks throughout the city of Ann Arbor!

We built this project in Unitystrong> using C# scriptingstrong> and a modular architectural approach to create interactive systems. We integrated the Mapbox SDKstrong> for location-based features and utilized free assets from Sketchfabstrong> and Mixamostrong> for our 3D models and animations.
