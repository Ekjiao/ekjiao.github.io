CSE Simulator

Have you ever wondered what life is like as a CS student attending the University of Michigan? Look no further than the CSE Simulator!

In this virtual experience, you will simulate the daily life of a CSE student visiting a computer lab! You can talk with friends, print objects, set off the fire alarm, and more!

Tools and Resources: We built this project in Unreal Engine using Blueprints and Behavioral Trees to create interactive systems. We used free assets from Sketchfab and Mixamo for our models and animations.
