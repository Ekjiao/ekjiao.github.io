VR Boxing Simulator

Explore your personal virtual gym designed to refine your boxing technique and sharpen your reflexes!

In this simulator, you can sharpen your technique in the ring with our boxing minigame or push your reaction speed to the limit with the popular reflex stick challenge!

We built this project in Unreal Engine using Blueprints and Behavioral Trees to create interactive systems. We used free assets from Sketchfab for our models.
